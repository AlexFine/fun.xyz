About

Crypto wallets today limit users to binary access control decisions. Smart wallets are primitive, and provide limited additional functionality.

Your alternative choice is to relinquish control to centralized parties. Though centralized custodial services can offer superior flexibility and convenience, a service providers’ negligence or insolvency can result in a complete loss of user funds. Custodial crypto solutions also limit ecosystem accessibility. The Odsy Network offers an alternative solution.

The Odsy Network introduces a new primitive onto a blockchain, decentralized multi-party-computation (dMPC). dMPC allows users to set granular access control policies and interact with multi-chain protocols in full-featured ways that were previously impossible. Projects can use Odsy to provide programmable access for any other blockchain, and offer users the flexibility of centralized solutions without sacrificing on security or decentralization.

The Odsy Network will enable DAO M&A, account recovery, fully compliant transactions at the smart-contract level, multi-chain DAOs and more. Fun.xyz is a product ecosystem building towards that future.
